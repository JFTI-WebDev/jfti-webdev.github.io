1. When indicated on the JFTI purchase order, suppliers must use JFTI’s customer-approved special process sources (anodize, heat treat, welding, etc.).

2. JFTI is to be contacted (by the supplier) in the event of nonconforming product/material. Arrangements for the approval of supplier nonconforming product/material must be as directed by a JFTI authorized manager or designee.
