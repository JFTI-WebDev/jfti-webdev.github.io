Electrical Fabrication Capabilities

Our electrical shop is staffed with certified technicians and modern equipment for the manufacturing and repair of cable products.


  • Multiple cable assembly tables and wiring jigs
  • Cable fabrication per AS50881 and IPC-620
  • Multi-pair harness fabrication tooling and experience
  • Single and twisted pair cable fabrication tooling and shielded and un-shielded pairs experience
  • Jacketed cable harness fabrication tooling and experience
  • Laser wire marking (direct marking) per AS5942 using Spectrum Technologies Capris 50-100 & Nova 800 wire marking machines. These machines are dedicated to marking aircraft wires and cables for aircraft manufacturing programs.
  • Cable harness identification (indirect marking) using Brady label printers, including continuous insulation sleeving printing. JFTI owns three Brady label printing machines.
  • Automatic wire stripping machines with preset program files that provide for quick set-up and change over for the Quick-Disconnect Umbilical differing wires sizes and types.
  • Extensive inventory of Daniels Manufacturing crimp dies, positioners, and crimp tools.
  • Tools and dies are calibrated per manufacture specifications on a quarterly schedule.
  • All electrical technicians have IPC J-STD-001 and IPC/WHMA-A-620C cable manufacturing training and certifications.
Additive Manufacturing

Our 3D printing lab is equipped with production-grade Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and prototype-grade FDM machine with ABS, PC-ABS, ULTEM 9085, Nylon 12 Carbon Fiber, and PEKK/Antero 800 capabilities. JF Taylor is highly experienced in additive manufacturing post-processing techniques for part smoothing and color and finish matching.

Facilities

In addition to our Cecil Mills Facility (CMF), J.F. Taylor has a significant footprint in Lexington Park, MD area servicing the NAS Patuxent River MD community. J.F. Taylor currently owns ten (10) facilities in the local area, including our CMF, Engineering Facility, Conference Center, and seven buildings in our Pax River Office Park. These facilities encompass a total of 280,000 sq.ft, of which 112,000 is Highbay/Mezzanine laboratory, manufacturing, and warehouse space. Currently, some of this space is used to support NAS Patuxent River MD development programs including unmanned trainers, manned flight trainers, part-task trainers, and software programs.
