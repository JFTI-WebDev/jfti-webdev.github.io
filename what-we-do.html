What we do

J.F. Taylor, Inc. Offers a Wide Variety of Services

At J.F. Taylor, we develop electrical, mechanical, and software components that are integrated into one seamless operational product. We design, fabricate, and integrate military technologies that include communication, navigation, computational, and other warfighter critical systems.


Software engineers and developers typically use C, C++, C#, and/or Python to develop applications for MS Windows or Linux operating systems and lower-level programming to develop FPGA solutions and other real-time embedded applications. Our software products include models to replicate avionics, aerodynamic, weapons, communication, and navigation systems. We also develop simulation systems software and instructor/operator simulation control software. Our technical experts develop visual and multispectral databases to immerse the trainees in a high-fidelity real-world simulation to maximize the training or testing event.


Our hardware engineers use Autodesk AutoCAD, PTC Creo, and PTC Windchill design tools to create complex solutions. Our fabrication capabilities bring those designs to life to support rapid prototyping solutions and large scale production.

Engineering and Professional Services

In the sky & on the ground

J.F. Taylor has a technically diverse workforce with the majority being degreed engineers or technical specialists with a broad base of knowledge in military aircraft and simulation systems. Our Engineering and Professional Services include the full spectrum of the systems engineering life-cycle, from conceptual design and system development, simulation and laboratory testing, flight testing, and data analysis to fleet introduction and support. We also specialize in hardware and software engineering solutions and developing test assets and data analysis tools to ensure systems meet performance requirements.

In addition to systems engineering, we staff simulation programs that fabricate and integrate high fidelity simulation systems to support testing, training, and operational readiness. To complement our engineering expertise, J.F. Taylor maintains a skilled staff of all disciplines required to successfully document, produce, and deliver products. Our professional services include Drafting, Information Technology, Logistics, Cost Analysis, Requirements Management, Scheduling, Documentation, and Facility Maintenance and Operations.

Design and Manufacturing

Where quality meets precision

J.F. Taylor is a successful hardware designer, developer, and integrator, with over 20 years of corporate knowledge and experience in electrical and mechanical engineering design and manufacturing. Our products, which include flight trainers, maintenance trainers, aircraft kits, UAS ground control stations and individual electro-mechanical components, are known for their quality of workmanship, operational reliability, and technical documentation packages.

At J.F. Taylor, we don’t just design, we also produce. In our manufacturing facilities, we have equipment and personnel that are experienced in mechanical and electro-mechanical fabrication, cable harness assembly and product organization, assembly, and delivery.

Software Development

Solutions for any problem

J.F. Taylor software engineers are experienced in the design, development, coding, and testing of software for new simulators, trainer upgrades, technology refresh programs, and aircraft operational software. Using higher order languages, and commercial and military interfaces (1553, ARINC, Ethernet, TCP/IP, DDS, and serial busses), our software engineers develop open architecture software solutions which pass relevant, accurate and timely data between aircraft systems and simulation components. Typical languages include C, C++, C#, FORTRAN, LUA, and PYTHON, in both Windows and Linux operating environments. J.F. Taylor software engineers research, analyze, derive and document system requirements, which are then used to develop the system design for the hardware/software solution. Our software efforts involve complex military avionics and sensor models, GUI driven Instructor Operator Station, HLA, DIS, and Live, Virtual, and Constructive (LVC) Distributed Exercises, and post-flight mission playback involving hi-fidelity Army, Navy, and Coast Guard flight simulators.

Simulation Training Products

For aircraft of all shapes and sizes

J.F. Taylor, Inc. produces and delivers advanced flight, maintenance, and weapons load training systems. Our vast engineering and manufacturing capabilities, coupled with our years of experience as a master hardware and software interface developer, make J.F. Taylor, Inc. a low-risk provider of training system devices. We integrate a multitude of vendors’ products into key trainer functions to produce superior products. We use a wide variety of computational systems, operating systems, and programming languages to meet our customers' requirements and deliver a highly reliable training system. Our latest systems are designed using PC technology with LINUX and Microsoft Windows operating systems. Our facilities house our engineering teams, production shops, and product support functions. Having an integrated facility lowers cost and risk as the technicians have direct access to the engineering team throughout the fabrication and installation process.

Our trainer products operation is certified to ISO 9001:2015 with Design.

Test Equipment

J.F. Taylor provides key technical design and integration support across the spectrum of flight Test Assets and Instrumentation. Since 1985, we have maintained resident expertise in designing and developing on-board instrumentation for precise time stamping of test events and post-mission reconstruction of event sequences across multiple aircraft platforms. The test data that we collect is critical for understanding system performance. We have developed standard and fighter-ruggedized Discrete Bus Formatter (DBF) systems for IFF events that record MIL-STD-1553 bus, ARINC bus, and GPS position data during flight test events. J.F. Taylor has designed, fabricated, and maintained the DBF II with hardware and software upgrades.

Aircraft/Maritime Modifications, Prototypes, and Components

At J.F. Taylor, we design, develop and integrate electrical and mechanical components, piece parts, and assemblies for aircraft and maritime systems. We have a core team with extensive engineering experience in electrical and mechanical component design. Our team also includes skilled “hands-on” professionals who are trained and certified to military standards for aircraft modifications/installs, mechanical fabrication, electromechanical fabrication, cable harness assembly and kit organization, assembly, and delivery. We have demonstrated our capabilities on numerous U.S. Navy and Marine Corps programs that include aircraft and maritime kits, aircraft modifications and kit installations, and engineering and fabrication of UAS ground control stations.
