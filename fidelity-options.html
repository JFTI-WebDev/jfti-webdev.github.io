Fidelity Options

Flight Control (FC) Product Series

J.F. Taylor manufactures three levels of simulation-grade flight controls to meet your simulation needs. All FC series grip shapes are accurate replications of the flight grips.

J.F. Taylor simulation-grade means:

  • Authentic replications with the proper form, fit, and function
  • Tough, rugged, and serviceable products
  • The correct level of fidelity to meet the application requirements

Fidelity Levels

FC0500

J.F. Taylor FC0500 series controls are compact and rugged desktop units. These controls offer full functionality in a small package at a lower price point. FC1000

Mid-fidelity devices are designed for desktop and or "task" cockpit applications where quality replicated flight grips are required with precision repeatable flight control movements. Most aircraft platforms are available in desktop and Dzus mounted options in this series.

FC2000

Mid-high fidelity devices for use in aircraft-specific applications. These devices accurately replicate the ergonomics and motions of real aircraft controls. Attach points that replicate the actual cockpit are available for most applications.

FC3000

The FC3000 series indicates any controls that utilize Bugeye's electronic control loading systems.
