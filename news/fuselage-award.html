JFTI Wins Flight Attendant and Fuselage Trainer Contract

November 19th, 2024

Great Mills, MD – [November 19, 2024] – JF Taylor, Inc. (JFTI) has been awarded an 18-month task order to develop the Flight Attendant Fuselage Trainer (FA FuT) for the USAF 89th Airlift Wing through the competitive Trainer Developer II (TDII) Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity contract. JFTI is a prominent DoD solutions provider, specializing in the development and delivery of advanced mission and training systems and solutions. In the past five years, JFTI has been awarded over $88 million under TDII contract.

Under the FA FuT program, the JFTI team will utilize its expertise to create advanced training devices for Flight Attendants operating USAF Executive Airlift aircraft. Two state-of-the-art training devices designed to train flight attendants for both Gulfstream (V/G-550) and Boeing (B-737 and B-757) aircraft will be provided to the 89th AW. “We are thrilled to have been awarded the FA FuT program contract and with the confidence that the warfighter puts in JFTI to deliver these trainers” said Chris Wasniak, JF Taylor COO. “This program highlights our dedication to delivering high-performance training solutions that enhance the capabilities of military personnel.”

With over 40 years of experience in design, engineering, and manufacturing, J.F. Taylor Inc. is a recognized global leader in superior training systems. Headquartered in Great Mills, Maryland, our commitment to innovation, quality, and customized solutions meets the diverse needs of defense clients across the various service branches. By focusing on advanced technology and leveraging our extensive expertise, JFTI develops training programs and systems that boost skill development, operational efficiency, and warfighter readiness.

For more information about JFTI's solutions, visit our website at www.jfti.com or contact bd@jfti.com.
