F-35 FC1000 Stick Mechanism

Professional flight simulation equipment for F-35 aircraft

Design Features:

  • Control stick feedback via springs
  • Control stick forces and movements (from neutral reference point)
  • PITCH -FWD/ AFT 10lbs ±0.5 lb@ 6.1°±1°
  • ROLL -RIGHT/LEFT 3 lbs±0.5 lb@ 5°±1°
  • Grip has the same fit, form and function as the aircraft
  • All switches, knobs, buttons, etc. are high fidelity replications of the actual aircraft
  • Unit I/O can be via Bugeye's standard USB/Ethernet card or discrete signals
  • Switches are individually replaceable
