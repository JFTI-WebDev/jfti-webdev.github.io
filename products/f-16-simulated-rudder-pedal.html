F-16 Simulated Rudder Pedal

Professional flight simulation equipment for F-16 aircraft

Rudder Pedal Features:

  • Pedal position adjusts in 1″ increments plus or minus 5″ (10″ total) from neutral position
  • Rudder pedal assembly comes with pedal adjust cable assembly (not shown here)
  • Yaw pedal rotational travel is ± 5° from neutral position
  • Toe brake rotational travel is 11° from neutral position
  • Yaw motion and toe brake motion send analog signals to a 9-pin connector
  • Fidelity: FC2000

Part Number: 24B120075-1001
