About the RAMT

The Bugeye RAMT is the next evolution in reconfigurable cockpit design. It has been designed for center stick and side stick aircraft, (shown here in F-35, F-15EX and T-38C) the RAMT is the most versatile platform ever designed. Can be adapted for rotary wing, cargo aircraft, air refueling aircraft or any fighter/trainer.

The Bugeye RAMT is a reconfigurable training platform with endless possibilities for the ever changing needs of our warfighters.
