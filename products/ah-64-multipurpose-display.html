AH-64 Multipurpose Display (MPD)

Advanced Simulation Display System

AH-64 Multipurpose Display (MPD) Features:

  • Bezel buttons, knobs and LCD display are all fully functional
  • The bezel and switches are all fully backlit and compatible with NVG
  • Switch I/O and displayed video images are controlled by the customer's host computer via BTIO50 USB/Ethernet card and DVI/VGA connections
  • Screen is 10.4" (diagonal) with a resolution of 1024×768
  • Viewing angle is 160° horizontal and vertical
  • DVI and VGA video connections are found on the back of the unit for video inputs
  • BTIO50 Interface card operates off of USB power
  • A GUI is provided with the BTIO50 for easy integration
  • Fidelity: FC2000

Part Number: 26B120436-1001
