Facilities

In addition to our Cecil Mills Facility (CMF), JFTaylor has a significant footprint in Lexington Park, MD area servicing the NAS Patuxent River MD community. JFTaylor currently owns ten (10) facilities in the local area, including our CMF, Engineering Facility, Conference Center, and seven buildings in our Pax River Office Park. These facilities encompass a total of 280,000 sq.ft, of which 112,000 is Highbay/Mezzanine laboratory, manufacturing, and warehouse space. Currently, some of this space is used to support NAS Patuxent River MD development programs including unmanned trainers, manned flight trainers, part-task trainers, and software programs.
