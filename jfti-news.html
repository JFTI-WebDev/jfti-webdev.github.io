November 19th, 2024

JF Taylor, Inc. (JFTI) has been awarded an 18-month task order to develop the Flight Attendant Fuselage Trainer (FA FuT) for the USAF 89th Airlift Wing through the competitive Trainer Developer II (TDII) Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity contract. JFTI is a prominent DoD solutions provider, specializing in the development and delivery of advanced mission and training systems and solutions. In the past five years, JFTI has been awarded over $88 million under TDII contract.

Under the FA FuT program, the JFTI team will utilize its expertise to create advanced training devices for Flight Attendants operating USAF Executive Airlift aircraft. Two state-of-the-art training devices designed to train flight attendants for both Gulfstream (V/G-550) and Boeing (B-737 and B-757) aircraft will be provided to the 89th AW.

“We are thrilled to have been awarded the FA FuT program contract and with the confidence that the warfighter puts in JFTI to deliver these trainers” said Chris Wasniak, JF Taylor COO. “This program highlights our dedication to delivering high-performance training solutions that enhance the capabilities of military personnel.”

With over 40 years of experience in design, engineering, and manufacturing, J.F. Taylor Inc. is a recognized global leader in superior training systems. Headquartered in Great Mills, Maryland, our commitment to innovation, quality, and customized solutions meets the diverse needs of defense clients across the various service branches. By focusing on advanced technology and leveraging our extensive expertise, JFTI develops training programs and systems that boost skill development, operational efficiency, and warfighter readiness.

For more information about JFTI's solutions, visit our website at www.jfti.com or contact bd@jfti.com.

April 1st, 2024

JF Taylor, Inc Expands Solutions Offering with Acquisition of Specialized USAF Contractor Clear Creek Applied Technologies

Great Mills, MD – [April 1, 2024] – JF Taylor announced today the acquisition of Clear Creek Applied Technologies (CCAT), a software solutions company with offices in Dayton, OH and Ft. Walton Beach, FL. CCAT specializes in mission-critical simulation and training solutions development as well as test and evaluation. This strategic alignment strengthens JF Taylor’s position as a leading provider of cutting-edge training systems and defense solutions to the Department of Defense (DoD) and its international partners. "We are excited to welcome CCAT and its talented team to the JF Taylor family," said Wayne Taylor, President. "CCAT 's proven expertise in developing and delivering high-quality and proven mission and training solutions aligns perfectly with our mission to provide our customers with the most advanced and comprehensive support capabilities. This acquisition broadens our portfolio and positions us to better serve the evolving needs of our customers in the digital age."

CCAT brings over 18 years of experience in the defense industry, specializing in the development and deployment of mission-critical software applications and T&E services for various US Navy, Air Force, and foreign partner programs. The company's team of highly skilled engineers, developers, and analysts boasts a deep understanding of its customers’ unique requirements and security protocols, ensuring the delivery of secure, reliable, and interoperable software solutions alongside mission critical test and evaluation services.

"Joining forces with JF Taylor presents an incredible opportunity to leverage our combined expertise and resources to make an even greater impact on the DoD's mission success," said Walt March, President/CEO at CCAT. "We are confident that this partnership will enable us to deliver even more innovative and effective solutions to our customers while expanding our reach and capabilities."

The acquisition of CCAT underscores JF Taylor’s commitment to providing its customers with a comprehensive suite of training and mission support solutions. By integrating CCAT's development capabilities with its existing expertise in highly complex military systems, JF Taylor will be able to offer a wider range of solutions that address the full spectrum of the warfighter’s training and operational needs.
