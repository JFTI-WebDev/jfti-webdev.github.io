    OUR HISTORY

    J.F. Taylor, Inc. Through the Years

    • 1983

      Flight Test Support

      J.F. Taylor's history began supporting the Naval Air Test Center by providing engineering and test support of Identification systems in Naval and Marine Corp aircraft.

    • 1984

      1st Prime Contract - IFF Flight Testing

    • 1986

      Data Reduction/Analysis

      J.F. Taylor developed their first flight-worthy data collection instrumentation, as well as a data reduction and analysis capability to support flight and laboratory testing.

    • 1986

      Laboratory Test Simulator

      J.F. Taylor designed, fabricated, and delivered a high-fidelity simulator/stimulator for the Patrol Avionics Test Laboratory, simulating the functionality of the P-3C sensor stations and the environment with which it operates.

    • 1991

      Full-Fidelity Simulation and Testing

      J.F. Taylor began its support of the Manned Flight Simulator, Simulation Division with the development and operation of high-fidelity simulations of multiple Navy and Marine Corp aircraft, providing avionics modeling, hardware-in-the-loop engineering, electrical and mechanical design, and fabrication.

    • 1997

      100 Employees

    • 1999

      High Fidelity Flight Trainer Products

      J.F. Taylor Trainer Products Group begins with the design, fabrication, and delivery of two AH-1W High Fidelity Flight Trainer to Taiwan. Our investments included the establishment of high-bay manufacturing space with mechanical and electrical fabrication tooling.

    • 2004

      ISO 9001 Certified

    • 2008

      Aircraft Cable Harnesses and Modification Kits

      J.F. Taylor turned their design and fabrication skills to support the fabrication of aircraft flight-worthy cable harnesses and electro-mechanical aircraft modification kits, with the delivery of CNS/ATM kits for the E-2C, C-2, and CH/MH-53.

    • 2012

      Unmanned & Maritime Systems

      J.F. Taylor began its support of unmanned and maritime systems with the engineering design support and fabrication of FireScout and UCAS control stations.

    • 2013

      AS9100 Certified

    • 2016

      1st Avionics System Contract - Mission Computer Alternative (MCA)

    • 2019

      500+ Employee Company

      Over the past 35 years, we have grown from a one-person operation to a company with over 500 employees and $150 million in annual revenues.

    • 2022

      New 95,000 Square Foot Facility

      On September 19, 2022, JF Taylor opened its Cecil's Mill Facility in Great Mills, MD which includes 60,000 square feet of high-bay, fabrication, and laboratory space. This one-of-a-kind facility in the NAS Patuxent River area supports over 100 electrical and mechanical fabricators and support personnel and over 50 engineers, software developers, and management personnel.

    Simulation & Training Systems

    Pilots trust us to help them learn

    Our products include manned and unmanned flight simulators that train today's pilots on the most advanced technologies in the world. We also design and manufacture trainers for Army ground vehicles, Navy shipboard systems, and military maintenance and weapons training systems.

    Avionics Systems & Aircraft Modifications

    J.F. Taylor designs, fabricates, and integrates military technologies that include communication, navigation, computational, and other warfighter critical systems for integration into fixed and rotary aircraft. In support of flight testing, we design, build, and install custom test and instrumentation equipment to record critical performance data. We also fabricate and install aircraft flight-worthy cables and kits, as well as performing other aircraft modifications.

    Unmanned & Maritime Systems

    J.F. Taylor designs, prototypes, tests, and fields complex Command, Control, Computers, Communications, Cyber, Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance systems within the US Navy.

    Ground-Based Systems

    J.F. Taylor designs, fabricates, and integrates military technologies that include communication, navigation, and other warfighter critical systems for installation into ground-based control systems, test facilities, and laboratories

    John F. Taylor, Sr.

    Founder & Chief Executive Officer

    John F. Taylor, Sr. is the company's founder and continues to serve as the Chief Executive Officer of J.F. Taylor. Mr. Taylor was the first person from his family to attend college, earning his Bachelor of Science in Physics from Providence College in 1961. He also holds a Degree of Electrical Engineering and Master of Science in Electrical Engineering from the U.S. Naval Post Graduate School, as well as a Master of Science in Administration of Science and Technology from George Washington University. Mr. Taylor served in the U.S. Navy for 20 years, retiring as a Commander in 1981. After retiring from the Navy, Mr. Taylor worked as a Program Manager at Electromagnetic Technology, Inc. (EMT) before founding J.F. Taylor, Inc.

    Wayne Taylor

    President

    Wayne Taylor was named President of J.F. Taylor in 2011. Prior to becoming President, Wayne served as Vice President of Business Operations for 20 years. Wayne earned his Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Towson University in 1986. Wayne started part-time in 1983, while still in college, and has been involved with J.F. Taylor since the company’s founding. For over 30 years at J.F. Taylor, Wayne has managed all aspects of business operations, including human resources, accounting, contracts, proposals, procurement, and facilities.

    John Taylor, Jr.

    Vice President, Engineering Services

    John Taylor, Jr. has been in a leadership role in engineering and program management since the company's founding in 1983. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech and graduated Magna Cum Laude. John was the catalyst to developing our hardware engineering expertise where he designed and developed real-time data recorders for flight testing. For the past 20 years, John has been in his role as Vice President responsible for all Acquisition and Test and Evaluation Services efforts.

    Chris Wasniak

    Vice President, Business Operations & Business Development

    As Vice President of Products Operations, Chris Wasniak is responsible for overseeing the management and implementation of complex military system solutions for a wide array of domestic and foreign military customers. As Vice President of Business Development, Chris is also responsible for creating and executing business development strategies and implementing corporate-wide initiatives to expand our business in target growth areas for all products and services. In addition, Chris serves as a Program Manager for high priority, product-based solutions. Chris holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Ohio University and an MBA from University of Maryland University College. He has 20 years of experience in the aerospace industry, including 8 years as Director of Engineering and Fabrication for a large defense contractor.

    Dave Sydnor
